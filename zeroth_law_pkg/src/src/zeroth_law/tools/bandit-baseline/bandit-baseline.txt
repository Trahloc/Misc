usage: bandit-baseline [-h] [-f {txt,html,json}] targets [targets ...]

Bandit Baseline - Generates Bandit results compared to a baseline

positional arguments:
  targets             source file(s) or directory(s) to be tested

options:
  -h, --help          show this help message and exit
  -f {txt,html,json}  specify output format

Additional Bandit arguments such as severity filtering (-ll) can be added and will be passed to Bandit.
